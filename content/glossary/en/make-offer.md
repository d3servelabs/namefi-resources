**Make Offer** is a domain listing format that invites prospective buyers to submit a bid, which the seller can accept, counter, or decline. Unlike a fixed [buy-it-now](/en/glossary/buy-it-now/) price, it suits names where the seller is unsure of the optimal number or expects an [end user](/en/glossary/end-user/) to surface who will pay above the investor floor. Sellers protect themselves with a [reserve price](/en/glossary/reserve-price/) — a private walk-away number below which they will not engage — and often route negotiation through a [domain broker](/en/glossary/domain-broker/) who reads buyer intent and anchors the conversation. The downside is friction and time: every sale becomes a negotiation.
