A **new gTLD** is one of the hundreds of [generic top-level domains](/en/glossary/gtld/) that [ICANN](/en/glossary/icann/) introduced through its expansion program, which began delegating strings like `.app`, `.xyz`, `.io`, `.dev`, and `.shop` from 2013 onward. The program opened the right-of-the-dot namespace beyond the original handful, letting brands and communities run their own [registry](/en/glossary/registry/) and giving registrants far more naming choice.
