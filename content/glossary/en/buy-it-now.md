**Buy It Now** (**BIN**) is a fixed, instant-purchase price on a domain listing: a buyer who pays the stated amount completes the sale immediately, with no negotiation. It is the simplest way to sell on the [aftermarket](/en/glossary/aftermarket/) — the price is transparent and the transaction is frictionless, which suits commodity names and sellers who already know their target number. The trade-off is that a firm price can leave money on the table when an [end user](/en/glossary/end-user/) would have paid more; for those cases sellers use a [make-offer](/en/glossary/make-offer/) flow instead, and many [marketplace](/en/glossary/marketplace/) platforms expose both at once.
