A **registrant** is the person or organization that holds the rights to a registered domain — the *registered name holder* on record. The registrant contracts with a [registrar](/en/glossary/registrar/), which in turn works with the [registry](/en/glossary/registry/) under [ICANN](/en/glossary/icann/) rules; the registrant is the closest thing the traditional system has to an "owner," though those rights live in a registrar account rather than something you hold directly.
