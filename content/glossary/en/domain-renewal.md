**Domain renewal** is paying to extend a domain's registration for another term — typically one year — before it lapses. Most [registrars](/en/glossary/registrar/) offer **auto-renew**, which charges your payment method automatically so the domain never reaches [expiration](/en/glossary/domain-expiration/); it is the simplest insurance against losing a name to an oversight. Renewal pricing is set by the [registry](/en/glossary/registry/) (plus the registrar's markup) and varies sharply by [TLD](/en/glossary/tld/).
