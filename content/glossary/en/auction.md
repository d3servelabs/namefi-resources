**Auctions** are competitive bidding processes used to determine market price and sell domains. **English auctions** start with a low price and bidders compete by raising bids until only one remains—common on platforms like eBay. **Dutch auctions** start with a high price that gradually decreases until someone accepts—ensuring quick sales at market-clearing prices. **Dynamic auctions** adjust their mechanics based on demand, participation, or other factors.
