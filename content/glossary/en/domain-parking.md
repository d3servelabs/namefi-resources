**Domain parking** is the practice of pointing an undeveloped domain to a page that displays pay-per-click ads, a search bar, or a for-sale notice rather than a full website. Owners park names to extract revenue while holding an asset they intend to sell, build on, or simply keep — turning an idle registration into a passive income stream. The revenue depends heavily on [type-in-traffic](/en/glossary/type-in-traffic/): visitors who navigate directly to the URL rather than arriving via a search engine. A parking service typically splits [PPC](/en/glossary/ppc/) ad earnings with the owner and may also display a [domain landing page](/en/glossary/domain-landing-page/) that routes interested buyers to the [aftermarket](/en/glossary/aftermarket/).
