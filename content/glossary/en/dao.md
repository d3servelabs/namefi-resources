A **DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization)** is an organization governed by [smart contracts](/en/glossary/smart-contract/) and community voting rather than traditional hierarchical management. DAOs allow members to collectively make decisions about treasury management, protocol upgrades, and strategic direction through token-based voting mechanisms. In the context of domains, DAOs can collectively own and manage valuable domain portfolios, make decisions about domain development or monetization, and govern domain-related protocols. Tokenized domains can be owned by DAOs, enabling communities to collectively invest in, develop, and govern domain assets through transparent, decentralized mechanisms.
