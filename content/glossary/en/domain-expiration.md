**Domain expiration** is the date a domain's registration term ends. Missing it does not instantly free the name: most [TLDs](/en/glossary/tld/) run a defined lapse sequence — a [grace period](/en/glossary/grace-period/) where you can still [renew](/en/glossary/domain-renewal/) at normal price, then a [redemption period](/en/glossary/redemption-period/) with a recovery fee, and finally [pending delete](/en/glossary/pending-delete/) before the name drops. [Registrars](/en/glossary/registrar/) send reminders, but the responsibility to renew is the registrant's.
