💡 Recommended Approach

For "safe" default states (Auto Renew ON, Privacy ON):

  • Ghost / Whisper - Ultra subtle, barely noticeable
  • Hint / Desaturated - Subtle but still recognizable
  • Soft (Egg Green) - Soft and comfortable egg green
  • Outline Thin - Refined thin border design

For "danger" states that need attention:

  • Use Red Prominent with glow - Immediately grabs attention
  • For critical warnings: Add pulsing animation
