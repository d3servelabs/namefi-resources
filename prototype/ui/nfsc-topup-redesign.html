ETH-only · amount denominated in USD value · ⓘ explains the credit

📋 Design intent

Problem
The old modal forced a DEX "swap" mental model and a parallel card checkout. Two flows, jargon ("Swap Tokens"), and a CTA that jumped between tabs.
Change
One ETH-only path. The subject is how much value (shown as $), not a raw NFSC figure. ⓘ defines NFSC on demand. ETH cost + gas stay visible; the CTA states the exact ETH amount.
Constraints
Mobile bottom-sheet, single floating action bar, theme tokens (no hardcoded zinc), RTL-safe, minimal chrome.
