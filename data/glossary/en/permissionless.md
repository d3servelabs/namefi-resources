**Permissionless** refers to systems that allow anyone to participate without requiring approval from a central authority. In blockchain contexts, permissionless networks let users transact, build applications, or interact with protocols without gatekeepers. Traditional domain management requires permission from [registrars](/r/en/glossary/registrar/) and follows their policies, but tokenized domains on Namefi enable permissionless transfers and integrations.
