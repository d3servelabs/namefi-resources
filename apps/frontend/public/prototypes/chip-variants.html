Cart Item — Option Selector Variants

Per-item domain setup controls (Auto Renew, Auto Park, Auto ENS, DNSSEC, Keep NS), each shown inside a cart-item-style card. Styled with the app's tokens via Tailwind CDN.

Design requirements

Problem
Let users configure per-item setup flags without bloating each cart row.
Goals
Compact, scannable, four flags + one import-only flag, clear on/off/disabled states.
Constraints
DNSSEC & Auto ENS disabled when the TLD lacks DNSSEC; Keep NS disables Auto Park/ENS/DNSSEC.
Success
A single tidy row, whole-control tooltips, no info icons, ~4–5 per row.

✨ Recommended — togglable chips (current)

check = on (primary fill), x = off (primary text), – = disabled (grey). Whole chip is the tooltip trigger. Try the configurator.

"AutoRenew" rendered on → off → disabled across styles & sizes.

💡 Recommendations

  • Default: solid togglable chips (A) — densest, clear three-state semantics, whole-chip tooltip.
  • Lower emphasis: outline (B) when the row shouldn't compete with price/CTA.
  • Avoid switches/checkboxes (E/F) here — they're taller and need separate labels, defeating the compact goal.
  • Add/remove (G) fits open-ended sets, not a fixed list of 4–5 known flags.
  • Collapsible (I) when the row should stay minimal by default: chips hidden behind a settings toggle, with an enabled-flags summary and the duration moved next to the price.
